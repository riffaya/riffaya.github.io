Accessing K-5 Science Resources

From Alameda County Office of Education (ACOE) Website

By Riffaya Rajid

Step 1: Website Address

Go to esp.acoe.org

This will directly take you to the Elementary Science Partnership website.

You will land on a page that looks like the image on the right.

Step 2: Click on "Instructional Strategies and Resources"

Click on the second image to access elementary science resources.

Step 3: Two Types of Science Resources

You will land a page that contains the following links:

  • Best Practice Instructional Strategies
  • Pedagogy Resources

The Best Practice Instructional Strategies link contains a large library of instructional strategies that can be sorted by Learning Goal, Science and Engineering Practice, and Phase in the 5E Learning Cycle.

The Pedagogy Resources link contains a variety of pedagogical resources such as articles, videos and sample lessons related to NGSS, 5E learning cycle, science misconcpetions, and assessments.

Let's first accses the Best Practice Instructional Strategies by clicking on the first of the two links shown on the image of the webpage on the right.

Step 4: Narrow Resource Search Results

Use the filters to narrow down the search results shown on the image of the webpage on the right.

You may not find any search results if you add too many filters.
Explore one filter at a time and then add more filters as needed.

Step 5: Toggling Between 2 Types of Resources

Let's navigate to Pedagogy Resources link.

Note that you can toggle between the two types of resources from the top panel.
